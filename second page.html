2.1 Onsite learning

  • Wear a mask when felling unwell
  • Sneeze into your arm or elbow
  • Always bring your own stationery, food, utelis, tumbler, mask and hand sanitizer

2.2 Greetings

  • Practice smiling, greeting, and showing respect to everyone in the school community
  • Greet your teachers at the beginning and end of each lesson

2.3 Communication

  • Communicate using proper english
  • Show respect to the person speaking
  • Request permission befor asking question in class

2.4 Responsibility

  • Arrive early and attend all classes accroding to the schedule
  • Wear the complete school uniform neatly and properly on school grounds
  • Maintain personal and environmenatl cleanliness
  • Take care personal belongings and use of school facilities appopriately
  • Submit all assignments on time
  • Respect the culture of waiting
  • Maintain order during school events

2.5 Permission

  • Use a hall pass when going to the restroom
  • Use a hall pass and fill in the off classroom form when leaving the class during lesson time
  • Complete all the off campus form when requesting to leave school during school hours

2.6 Examinations

  • Students are required to take all exams according to scheduled time
  • Students must comply with all exam regulation
  • Students who miss exams without valid reason will receive a maximum score of the Minimum Mastery Criteria (KKM), unless they are about to submit due to illness (with a doctor note) or a bereavement

2.7 Worship Time

  • Students must respect worship time by giving full attention during the session.
  • Students are required to take notes during sermons or devotions and submit them to their mentor
  • Students are not allowed to chat, study, or do homework during worship
  • Students are not allowed to disturb others during worship
  • Students must bring a printed Bible when attending worship

In line with the vision, mission, values of Elyon Christian School, we expect every student to reach their full potential and apply the behavioural standards outlined above.
